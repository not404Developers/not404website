Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of your project. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex sites or web applications can take 8-12 weeks or longer. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a specific timeline based on your requirements.

What information do you need to get started?

To kickstart your project, we typically need your business information, brand guidelines (if available), content for the website, and a clear understanding of your goals and target audience. We'll guide you through this process with our detailed project questionnaire.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates. We can customize a maintenance plan based on your specific needs.

Can you help with website content creation?

Absolutely! We have a team of professional copywriters who can create engaging and SEO-friendly content for your website. We also offer professional photography and video production services to enhance your visual content.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our websites are built with a mobile-first approach. We ensure that your website looks and functions perfectly on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. Responsive design is standard in all our projects.

How do you handle website security?

Security is a top priority for us. We implement various security measures including SSL certificates, secure hosting environments, regular security updates, firewall protection, and secure coding practices. For e-commerce sites, we ensure PCI compliance for secure payment processing.

Do you offer hosting services?

Yes, we provide reliable hosting solutions tailored to your website's needs. Our hosting services include regular backups, 99.9% uptime guarantee, technical support, and scalable resources to accommodate your growing traffic. We can also work with your existing hosting provider if you prefer.
